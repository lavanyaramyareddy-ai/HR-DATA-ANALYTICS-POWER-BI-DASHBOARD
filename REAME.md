# HR Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This HR Analytics Dashboard was developed using Power BI to analyze employee data and provide insights into workforce performance, employee attrition, job satisfaction, and organizational trends. The dashboard helps HR professionals make data-driven decisions to improve employee retention and workforce management.

## Objectives

* Monitor employee attrition rates.
* Analyze workforce demographics.
* Track employee satisfaction levels.
* Identify factors contributing to employee turnover.
* Support strategic HR decision-making.

## Tools Used

* Power BI
* Power Query
* DAX
* Data Modeling
* Data Visualization

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Employees
* Attrition Count
* Attrition Rate
* Active Employees
* Average Employee Age
* Job Satisfaction Score

## Dashboard Features

* Interactive filters and slicers.
* Department-wise attrition analysis.
* Gender and age distribution insights.
* Job role and education field analysis.
* Employee satisfaction and performance tracking.
* Dynamic visualizations and KPI cards.

## Insights Generated

* Identified departments with high attrition rates.
* Analyzed employee turnover by age, gender, and job role.
* Evaluated job satisfaction trends across the organization.
* Highlighted areas for improving employee engagement and retention.

## Project Outcome

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of workforce metrics, enabling HR teams to make informed decisions, reduce attrition, and enhance employee satisfaction.
